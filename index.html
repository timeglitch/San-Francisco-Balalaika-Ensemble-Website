About Us

The San Francisco Balalaika Ensemble performs on balalaikas of various sizes from prima to kontra-bass; plus domras of various sizes from small to bass. Also present are Russian and Ukrainian folk woodwinds zhalieka and brelka (Russian single and double reeds), svirel (Russian flute) rozhok (Russian wooden trumpet) sopilka (Ukrainian flute), accordion and bayan (Russian button accordian) and violin (for Ukrainian music).
